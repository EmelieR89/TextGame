Get out!

You wake up. Your head is pounding and you're nauseous. You put your hand on your forehead, blood and a big bump. Everything is a blur. What happened yesterday? What time is it? What is this place? You look around. Nobody’s there. “Hello?” No answer, dead silence. Then, in the distance you here heavy footsteps. The panic kicks in. You need to get out…
